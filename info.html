Heart Rate, Pulse or Heart Beat?

What is the difference between Heart Rate, Pulse and Heart Beat?

Heart Rate is a quantitative measure, it is the number of times a minute that our heart contracts or beats (expressed in bpm).

Pulse is a direct measure of Heart Rate, it can be feelt in the arteries with finguers. In this way you feel different blood pressure at the neck and at the wrist.

Heart Beat is a qualitative measure, it has a rythm, a tempo. With Heart Beats, you can check your Heart Rate Variability.
